CHARACTERS

BLUE CAT

Blue Cat, aka “Blue”, is one of the most beloved residents in Sapphire Town. He is a genuine cat, shy and reserved at first, until he opens up to share his charismatic side. He believes kindness is power and this fuels his friendships and interactions.


Blue enjoys kickstarting his days by waking up early to watch the clouds. He spends his free time drawing, dreaming, and gaming. When he’s not gazing at the sky, gaming, or sketching, Blue can be found mixing delicious beverages at The Milk Chug with his chaotic sidekick, Chugs.




ARDI

Ardi is Blue Cat’s anteater friend, known for his sharp intellect and abrasive demeanor. He has a short temper and little patience for anything he perceives as incompetence. Every now and then, he reveals his playful side with his close friends.


Ardi is passionate about technology and thrives in solitude at his workshop in Sapphire Town. He enjoys the occasional adventure and staying fueled by caffeine from his visits to The Milk Chug.




CHUGS

Chugs is Blue Cat’s sidekick. He sprang to life from a milk carton Blue purchased one day and they’ve been inseparable ever since. Chugs is charming and curious, loves adventure, and is determined to take on any challenge that comes his way. Chugs spends his time crafting perfect brews at The Milk Chug and is cherished by all the residents of Sapphire Town.




JO

Jo is good friends with Blue Cat. She is a radiant red bird and fashionista, always looking for fresh and fun ways to style herself and her friends. Jo has a knack for adapting to new situations and making friends in all who encounter her.


Feathered Fashions is where she spends her time sewing and designing what inspires her. In her free time, Jo enjoys gardening, singing, and spending time with friends. She brings joy and light to all the residents of Sapphire Town.
